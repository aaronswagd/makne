GERO Arquitectos

A brand identity for a Juárez-based architecture studio that blends serious elegance with Mexican artisanal tradition.

Overview

GERO Arquitectos is a new studio specializing in residential renovations and property development. They approached us to create a brand identity that reflected their dual commitment: professional architectural rigor and warm Mexican craftsmanship.

Their signature element — concrete and brick latticework — became the creative foundation for the entire visual system. The result is a mark that feels structural yet artisanal, modern yet deeply rooted.

Brand Strategy

Personality

Serious and elegant, yet warm. A studio that values structure as much as soul. Professional but never cold.

Audience

Homeowners seeking renovations and property investors looking for distinctive, well-designed spaces.

Direction

Minimalist with artisanal accents. Concrete textures, warm neutrals, and geometric patterns inspired by latticework.

Core Differentiator

"Mexican craft meets architectural precision." Custom concrete and brick latticework that no other studio in Juárez offers.

Color System

Inspired by raw construction materials and the warm light of northern México. A palette of golden sand, warm clay, and earthy green.
