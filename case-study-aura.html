Aura Beauty

A complete brand identity for a luxury skincare line — from logo to packaging, every detail whispers sophistication and purity.

Overview

Aura Beauty approached us to create a brand identity that reflected the purity of their ingredients and the luxury of their formulations. The challenge was to design a system that felt both clinical and warm — premium but not pretentious.

We developed a minimalist visual language rooted in soft neutrals, generous whitespace, and refined typography. The result is a brand that communicates trust, elegance, and quiet confidence.

Brand Strategy

Personality

Calm, refined, intentional. A brand that doesn't shout — it whispers and people lean in.

Audience

Discerning women aged 28–45 who value ingredient transparency, sustainability, and understated luxury.

Direction

Minimalist editorial. Soft light. Natural textures. Generous negative space. Typography-led compositions.

Core Idea

"Science meets softness." Clinical precision wrapped in warmth and human touch.
