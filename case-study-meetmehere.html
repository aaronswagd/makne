Meet Me Here

Magazine layout and art direction for an independent publication based in the United States.

Overview

Meet Me Here Magazine is an independent publication that explores place, identity, and culture through long-form storytelling. I was brought on to design article layouts that balanced immersive photography with clean, readable typography.

The goal was to create a visual rhythm that carried the reader through each story — bold headlines, generous whitespace, and carefully placed imagery that complemented the narrative without interrupting it.

Industry Publishing / Media
Services Magazine Layout, Art Direction, Typography

Deliverables Full Article Layouts, Cover Design, Typography System

Design Approach

Tone

Warm, inviting, contemplative. A magazine that feels like a slow Sunday morning with a cup of coffee.

Audience

Curious readers aged 25–50 who value storytelling, travel, culture, and thoughtful photography.

Direction

Editorial minimalism. Strong photography, elegant type, and generous negative space that lets content breathe.

Core Idea

"Every place has a story." Design that honors the narrative without overpowering it.
